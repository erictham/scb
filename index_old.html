Strategic Prospecting for Transactional Banking

This serves as a prototype for a holistic view of transactional data from both SCB and Thomson Reuters. There are four key business applications for TR's BOLD product - TR Intelligent Tagging & DataFusion.
  • 1. The first is to identify key hub and spokes in a transactional network through graphical analytical techniques. For example, most corporates operate through key trading hub companies, with multiple transactions within its group and other entities. This application allows visualisation of the transactional network, identifying the key hub important for prospecting. In addition, Eikon information for the entities are integrated into the system.
  • 2. Targets 'omitted' prospects through the TR supply chain. These 'omitted' prospects are already banked by SCB, but through the existing TR's supply network enables viewing of their trade deals with other counterparties as well.
  • 3. News analytics: flagging of key events and relationships involving prospects through scanning of unstructured texts. These are scanned bt TRIT and key events eg. debt financing, M&A, deals financing etc. are notified for a holistic view of the prospects.

Proof of Concept for Standard Chartered Bank
The DataFusion allows users to see relationships in their data. In this PoC from Thomson Reuters and the SCB data science teams, SCB's transactional data is merged with TR"s wealth of company data to show the extensive relationships observed in the transactional network. TR"s wealth of data includes specific company (NTBs) information as well as its proprietary supply chain network that is curated from filings, industry articles and trusted news sources.
